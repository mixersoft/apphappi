About Snaphappi
Our mission is to take the “work” out of family photos and make it “play”.

AppHappi was created to offer you a quick, easy, and free "bite" from our “apple” (a.k.a rated photos). We know you don't have the time or patience to organize and rate your photos - but by making a game of it, maybe you do.

We believe your photos are not a To-do list, something to finish and forget. Instead, they are there to re-live and enjoy every day. Have fun with it!
